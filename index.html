05Are there arrival card requirements for entering Singapore?+
Yes — two digital registrations are required:
  • SG Arrival Card (SGAC): Must be completed within 3 days before arrival at Singapore. Free to fill out at eservices.ica.gov.sg/sgarrival-card. This replaces the old paper disembarkation card.

Clients who forget to fill the SG Arrival Card may be asked to complete it at the airport, causing delays. Remind them to do it on their phone 1–3 days before travel.
Filipinos enjoy visa-free entry to Singapore. No visa required.

10What local tips and reminders should I share with clients before they depart?+
  • No chewing gum — banned in Singapore. Customs may confiscate it on arrival.
  • No durian in MRT or taxis — strictly prohibited and fined.
  • Jaywalking is illegal — use crossings. Fines are real.
  • Littering = heavy fine. Keep it clean.
  • Hawker centres are the best value food option — Lau Pa Sat, Maxwell Food Centre, and Old Airport Road are popular.
  • MRT is the easiest way to get around — buy an EZ-Link card (approx SGD 10 including SGD 5 credit) at any MRT station upon arrival.
  • Tap water is safe to drink.
  • Weather: Expect afternoon thundershowers especially in Oct–Dec. Bring an umbrella.

09What local tips should I share with clients?+
  • Traffic is intense — crossing the street requires confidence. Look for natural gaps in the motorbike flow and walk steadily. Hesitating is more dangerous.
  • Grab is the safest way to get around — download the app before arrival. Avoid unmarked taxis.
  • Bargaining is expected at Ben Thanh Market — start at 40–50% of the asking price.
  • Drink only bottled water. Never drink tap water.
  • Street food is safe and delicious — look for stalls with lots of locals. Banh mi, pho, and banh xeo are must-tries.
  • Exchange currency at local gold shops (tiem vang) for best rates, not airport counters. USD is widely accepted.
  • ATMs are widely available. Notify your bank before travel to avoid card blocks.

09What local tips should I share with Japan-bound clients?+
